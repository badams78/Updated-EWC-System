# Broadway Shows - January 2026

## NEW SHOWS (Opening January 2026)

### Bug ⭐ NEW TO BROADWAY
- **Opening:** January 8, 2026
- **Theater:** Samuel J. Friedman Theatre
- **Starring:** Carrie Coon, Namir Smallwood
- **Written by:** Tracy Letts (Tony & Pulitzer Prize winner)
- **Directed by:** David Cromer (Tony winner for The Band's Visit)
- **About:** A psychological thriller making its Broadway debut after 30 years. An unexpected, intense romance between a lonely waitress and a mysterious drifter in a seedy Oklahoma motel room twists into something far more dangerous.
- **Note:** First Broadway show to open in 2026!
- **Age:** 14+ (contains violence, drug use, profanity, nudity)

## TOP-RATED LONG-RUNNING SHOWS

### Wicked
- **Theater:** Gershwin Theatre
- **About:** Three-time Tony winner featuring iconic songs "Defying Gravity" and "Popular." Modern Broadway's biggest original musical telling the untold story of the witches of Oz.
- **Great for:** Musical lovers, first-time Broadway goers

### The Lion King
- **Theater:** Minskoff Theatre
- **About:** Broadway's third longest-running show. Julie Taymor's breathtaking production with stunning puppetry and costumes. Tony winner for Best Musical.
- **Great for:** All ages, visual spectacle lovers

### Hamilton
- **Theater:** Richard Rodgers Theatre
- **About:** Lin-Manuel Miranda's groundbreaking hip-hop musical about Founding Father Alexander Hamilton. Multiple Tony Award winner.
- **Great for:** History buffs, innovative theater fans

### Hadestown
- **Theater:** Walter Kerr Theatre
- **About:** Anaïs Mitchell's Tony Award-winning mythic musical - a hell-raising, heart-breaking journey to the underworld retelling the Orpheus and Eurydice myth.
- **Great for:** Folk/indie music fans, unique theater experiences

### MJ the Musical
- **Theater:** Neil Simon Theatre
- **About:** Explores Michael Jackson's life and stratospheric rise to fame, featuring his greatest hits and iconic choreography.
- **Great for:** Michael Jackson fans, dance enthusiasts

### Six
- **Theater:** Lena Horne Theatre
- **About:** Pop concert-style musical featuring the six wives of Henry VIII reclaiming their stories.
- **Great for:** Pop music fans, girl power themes

### Chicago
- **Theater:** Ambassador Theatre
- **About:** The longest-running American musical in Broadway history. Jazz-age tale of murder, corruption, and "all that jazz."
- **Great for:** Classic musical theater fans

### Aladdin
- **Theater:** New Amsterdam Theatre
- **About:** Disney's magical musical featuring the Genie, flying carpet, and beloved songs.
- **Great for:** Families, Disney fans

### Mamma Mia! (Returned to Broadway!)
- **Theater:** TBD
- **About:** ABBA jukebox musical back on Broadway after a decade! Feel-good story set on a Greek island.
- **Great for:** ABBA fans, fun night out

### Stranger Things: The First Shadow ⭐ NEW
- **Theater:** Marquis Theatre
- **About:** The Netflix phenomenon comes to Broadway! This prequel is set in 1959 Hawkins, Indiana and reveals the origins of the Upside Down. Discover how a young Jim Hopper, Joyce Byers, and Bob Newby's lives intertwined with the mysterious Henry Creel.
- **Great for:** Stranger Things fans, sci-fi/thriller lovers, immersive theatrical experiences
- **Note:** Spectacular stage effects and a gripping story even if you haven't seen the show
