# NYC Events - January 2026

## FESTIVALS & SPECIAL EVENTS

### NYC Winter Outing (January 20 - February 12, 2026)
**The best time to visit!**
- 2-for-1 tickets to Broadway shows
- Restaurant Week deals
- Discounted museum admissions
- Hotel packages
- **Website:** nycgo.com

*Your trip falls perfectly within this event - take advantage of the deals!*

---

### Under the Radar Festival (January 7-25, 2026)
- One of NYC's most exciting theater and performance festivals
- Over 20 venues across the city
- Features dance, music, theater, film, opera, and experimental works
- NY-based artists: Narcissister, The HawtPlates, Kaneza Schaal, Lisa Fagan, Lena Engelstein
- European performers: Cherish Menzo, Mario Banushi
- **Tip:** Great for adventurous theater-goers looking beyond Broadway

---

### Winter Jazzfest (January 8-13, 2026)
- Spectacular celebration of jazz in Manhattan and Brooklyn
- Incredible lineup of artists
- Keynote speakers and panel discussions
- Manhattan and Brooklyn marathons with jazz concerts
- Multiple venues from 5pm to 3am
- **Website:** winterjazzfest.com

## FREE & LOW-COST EVENTS

### MoMA Free Friday Nights
- **When:** Every Friday, 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM
- **Who:** Free for New York State residents
- **Tip:** Book in advance through "UNIQLO Free Friday Night" program

### Morgan Library & Museum Free Fridays
- **When:** Every Friday, 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- **Tip:** Reservations required one week in advance

### Central Park Winter Activities
- Ice skating at Wollman Rink
- Winter walks through the park
- Hot chocolate at The Loeb Boathouse

## UNIQUE EXPERIENCES

### Carreau Club Pétanque (Industry City, Brooklyn)
- French boules sport (similar to bocce ball)
- Nine indoor pétanque courts
- Perfect winter activity
- Fun, social atmosphere with food and drinks

### Ice Skating
- **Wollman Rink** (Central Park) - Classic NYC experience
- **The Rink at Rockefeller Center** - Iconic but crowded
- **Bryant Park Winter Village** - Free admission (skate rental extra)
- **Prospect Park LeFrak Center** (Brooklyn)

## HOLIDAY CARRYOVER (Early January)

If visiting in early January, you might still catch:
- Holiday window displays at department stores
- Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree (usually through early January)
- Holiday lights in various neighborhoods

---

## WEATHER TIPS FOR LATE JANUARY

**Expect:**
- Temperatures: 25-40°F (-4 to 4°C)
- Possible snow
- Short daylight hours (sunset around 5:00 PM)

**Pack:**
- Warm layers
- Winter coat, hat, gloves, scarf
- Waterproof boots
- Hand warmers (nice to have!)

**Indoor backup plans are essential!**
