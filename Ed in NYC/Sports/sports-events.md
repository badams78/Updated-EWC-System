# NYC Sports Events - Last Week of January 2026

## Overview

January is peak season for both NBA basketball and NHL hockey in NYC. The city has multiple professional teams playing home games throughout the month.

---

## NYC SPORTS TEAMS

### Basketball (NBA)

#### New York Knicks
- **Arena:** Madison Square Garden (Midtown Manhattan)
- **About:** NYC's beloved NBA team, one of the league's most iconic franchises
- **Experience:** MSG is "The World's Most Famous Arena" - an electric atmosphere for any game
- **Tip:** Knicks games are popular; book tickets in advance

#### Brooklyn Nets
- **Arena:** Barclays Center (Brooklyn)
- **About:** Brooklyn's NBA team with a modern arena and great sightlines
- **Experience:** Easier to get tickets than Knicks; great food options at Barclays
- **Tip:** Take the subway to Atlantic Ave-Barclays Ctr station

---

### Hockey (NHL)

#### New York Rangers
- **Arena:** Madison Square Garden (Midtown Manhattan)
- **About:** Original Six NHL franchise with passionate fanbase
- **Experience:** Fast-paced, exciting action; great for first-time hockey viewers
- **Tip:** Rangers games often sell out - book early

#### New York Islanders
- **Arena:** UBS Arena (Elmont, NY - near Belmont Park)
- **About:** Four-time Stanley Cup champions with devoted Long Island fanbase
- **Experience:** Brand new state-of-the-art arena (opened 2021)
- **Tip:** Accessible via LIRR to Elmont station

#### New Jersey Devils
- **Arena:** Prudential Center (Newark, NJ)
- **About:** Three-time Stanley Cup champions
- **Experience:** Easy PATH train ride from Manhattan; often more affordable tickets
- **Tip:** Good option if Rangers/Islanders are sold out

---

## MADISON SQUARE GARDEN

Even if you're not a sports fan, catching a game at MSG is a quintessential NYC experience.

**Address:** 4 Pennsylvania Plaza, New York, NY 10001

**Getting There:**
- Subway: A/C/E, 1/2/3, B/D/F/M to 34th St-Penn Station
- Directly above Penn Station

**Tips:**
- Arrive early to explore the arena
- Food options inside are pricey - eat beforehand
- No bags larger than 12"x12"x6"
- Check the schedule at msg.com for game times
