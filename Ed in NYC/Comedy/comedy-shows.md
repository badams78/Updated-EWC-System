# NYC Comedy Shows - January 2026

## LEGENDARY VENUES

### Comedy Cellar
**The "Harvard of Comedy Clubs"**

- **Address:** 117 MacDougal Street, Greenwich Village
- **Website:** comedycellar.com
- **Shows:** 7 nights a week, multiple shows nightly

**Why It's Special:**
- Where comedians like Jon Stewart, Aziz Ansari, Amy Schumer, Chris Rock, Kevin Hart, and Dave Chappelle got their start
- A-list comedians still drop in regularly for surprise sets
- Intimate basement setting with incredible energy
- You never know who might show up!

**Booking Tips:**
- Reserve online in advance - shows sell out
- Arrive early for best seating
- 2-drink minimum
- No photography/recording allowed
- Shows typically 75-90 minutes

**Show Times:**
- Usually 7:30pm, 9:30pm, 11:30pm (and sometimes more)

---

### Village Underground
**Comedy Cellar's Sister Club**

- **Address:** 130 West 3rd Street, Greenwich Village
- **About:** Same ownership as Comedy Cellar, comedy 7 nights a week
- **Tip:** Easier to get into than Comedy Cellar; same quality comedians

### New York Comedy Club
**Authentic NYC Stand-Up Experience**

- **Locations:** East Village & Gramercy (flagship)
- **Website:** newyorkcomedyclub.com
- **Shows:** Multiple shows daily, January 2026 calendar available

**Notable Shows:**
- "Good Eggs" - Called "one of the best weekly shows in the city" by TimeOut NY

## BOOKING & TICKET TIPS

**General Advice:**
- Book Comedy Cellar 1-2 weeks in advance
- Most clubs have 2-drink minimum
- Arrive 20-30 minutes early
- Late shows (11:30pm+) tend to be raunchier
- Weekends are busiest; weeknights more intimate

**Pricing:**
- Cover: $15-40 depending on venue/night
- Drinks: $8-15 each
- Budget ~$50-75 per person total

**What to Expect:**
- Shows run 60-90 minutes
- Usually 4-6 comedians per show
- Don't sit in front row unless you're okay being picked on!
