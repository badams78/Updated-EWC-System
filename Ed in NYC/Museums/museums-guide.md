# NYC Museums Guide - January 2026

## THE BIG THREE

### The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met)
**The world's largest art museum**

- **Address:** 1000 Fifth Avenue (at 82nd St)
- **Hours:** Sun-Tue, Thu 10am-5pm; Fri-Sat 10am-9pm; Closed Wed
- **Admission:** $30 adults, $22 seniors, $17 students; Free for kids under 12
- **Tip:** Pay-what-you-wish for NY State residents

**Current Exhibits (January 2026):**
- "Emily Sargent: Portrait of a Family" (through March 8, 2026)
- "Making It Modern: European Ceramics" (through June 14, 2026)
- "Iba Ndiaye: Between Latitude and Longitude" (through May 31, 2026)
- "A Passion for Jade: The Bishop Collection" (through June 28, 2026)
- "Embracing Color: Enamel in Chinese Decorative Arts" (through June 28, 2026)
- "Colorful Korea: The Lea R. Sneider Collection" (through Feb 15, 2026)
- "Fanmania" (through May 12, 2026)
- "Afterlives: Contemporary Art in the Byzantine Crypt" (through Jan 10, 2027)

**Must-See Permanent Collections:**
- Egyptian Temple of Dendur
- European Paintings (Vermeer, Rembrandt, Monet)
- American Wing
- Rooftop Garden (seasonal)

---

### MoMA (Museum of Modern Art)
**World's premier modern art museum**

- **Address:** 11 West 53rd Street (Midtown)
- **Hours:** Daily 10:30am-5:30pm; Fri until 8pm
- **Admission:** $30 adults, $22 seniors, $17 students; Free for kids under 16
- **FREE:** Friday nights 5:30-8pm for NY State residents (advance booking required)

**Current Exhibits (January 2026):**
- "Ruth Asawa: A Retrospective" (through Feb 7, 2026)
- "Helen Frankenthaler: A Grand Sweep" (through Feb 8, 2026)
- "Wifredo Lam: When I Don't Sleep, I Dream" (through April 11, 2026)
- "Face Value: Celebrity Press Photography" (through June 21, 2026)
- "Odili Donald Odita: Songs from Life" (through Spring 2026)
- "Sasha Stiles: A LIVING POEM" (through Spring 2026)

**Must-See Permanent Works:**
- Van Gogh's "Starry Night"
- Picasso's "Les Demoiselles d'Avignon"
- Warhol's "Campbell's Soup Cans"
- Monet's "Water Lilies"

---

### Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum
**Frank Lloyd Wright's architectural masterpiece**

- **Address:** 1071 Fifth Avenue (at 89th St)
- **Hours:** Sun-Wed, Fri 11am-6pm; Sat 11am-8pm; Closed Thu
- **Admission:** $30 adults, $25 military, $19 seniors/students; Free for kids under 12

**Current Exhibits (January 2026):**
- "Rashid Johnson: A Poem for Deep Thinkers" (through Jan 18, 2026)
- "Gabriele Münter: Contours of a World" (through April 26, 2026)

**The Experience:**
- The building itself is the star - spiral up the famous rotunda
- View art while walking the continuous ramp
- Great museum shop

## OTHER NOTABLE MUSEUMS

### Morgan Library & Museum
- **Address:** 225 Madison Avenue (at 36th St)
- **Special Exhibit:** "Renoir Drawings" (through Feb 8, 2026) - First show like this since Paris in 1921!
- **FREE:** Fridays 5-8pm (reservations required 1 week in advance)
- **Tip:** Beautiful historic library, perfect for bookworms

### American Museum of Natural History
- **Address:** Central Park West at 79th St
- **Highlights:** Dinosaur halls, planetarium, dioramas, new Gilder Center
- **Admission:** Pay-what-you-wish for NY residents
- **Great for:** All ages, especially if it's cold outside

### Whitney Museum of American Art
- **Address:** 99 Gansevoort Street (Meatpacking District)
- **Focus:** American art from 20th century to present
- **Tip:** Amazing outdoor terraces with Hudson River views

### Brooklyn Museum
- **Address:** 200 Eastern Parkway, Brooklyn
- **Special Exhibit:** "Monet and Venice" (through Feb 1, 2026)
- **Admission:** Pay-what-you-wish
- **Tip:** Less crowded than Manhattan museums

### MoMA PS1 (Queens)
- **Address:** 22-25 Jackson Ave, Long Island City
- **Current:** "Vaginal Davis: Magnificent Product" (through March 2, 2026)
- **Admission:** $10 adults; FREE for NYC residents
- **Tip:** Experimental, cutting-edge contemporary art

## MONEY-SAVING TIPS

**Free/Discount Options:**
- MoMA Free Fridays (5:30-8pm, NY State residents)
- Morgan Library Free Fridays (5-8pm)
- The Met pay-what-you-wish (NY State residents)
- Brooklyn Museum pay-what-you-wish
- NYC Winter Outing deals (Jan 20 - Feb 12)

**CityPASS:**
- Includes Met, MoMA, Natural History + more
- Saves money if visiting 3+ attractions
